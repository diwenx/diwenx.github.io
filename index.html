About me

I am a fifth year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Michigan. My research focuses on areas where the privacy, security and availability implications of networked systems affect users in the real world. I conduct Internet measurements at scale, use those observations to refine threat models, and build countermeasures to safeguard users’ communication on this increasingly adversarial Internet.
